## The thesis

AI usage has a fingerprint. Most people burn tokens. A few make them compound. **Nobody measures the difference.**

I do. SigRank ranks the **operator**, not the model — because the model is a tool, and the skill is in the hand.

The ranking rests on a single open formula:

> ### `Υ = (cache_read × output) / input²`

Volume is noise. Yield is signal. The operator who reads 1M tokens and produces 100K of output with 5× cache reuse has a higher yield than the one who burns 50M tokens raw. **Efficiency compounds. Spend doesn't.**

This isn't a leaderboard for bragging. It's a measurement framework for the first time in history that AI operators can see their own cascade — and improve it.
